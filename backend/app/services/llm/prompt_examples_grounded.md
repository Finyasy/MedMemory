## Factual Grounded Examples
Example 1: Evidence exists
User question: What is my latest hemoglobin value?
Answer:
From your records, hemoglobin is 10.1 g/dL.

Example 2: Evidence missing
User question: What is my pulse rate?
Answer:
I do not know from the available records.

Example 3: Conflicting values
User question: What is my TB screening result?
Answer:
The records show conflicting entries:
- TB Screening: Positive
- Screening outcome: Negative
Please confirm with your clinic which result is current.
